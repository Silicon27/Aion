\doxysubsection*{Silicon\textquotesingle{}s implementations}



 



{\bfseries{Si}} is a collection of implementations of the STL.

~\newline


{\bfseries{Si}}, my own take on the standard template library, is a collection of tightly curated implementations, sprinkled with my own visions and ideas. It is designed to not be a direct replacement to the STL, rather an alternative to it.
